The **Convergence Design Lab** (formerly C Design Lab) at Purdue University creates the future of design by converging human creativity, computation, and making. Directed by [Prof. Karthik Ramani](https://engineering.purdue.edu/ME/People/ptProfile?resource_id=1445), the lab sits at the intersection of mechanical engineering, human-computer interaction, and artificial intelligence.

Our [research](/projects/) spans six core areas: **AR/VR/MR** for immersive skill transfer, **AI & computer vision** for design and manufacturing, **human-shape interaction**, **geometric deep learning** on 3D representations, **smart product design**, and **design learning** platforms that democratize engineering education.

The lab has produced [260+ publications](/publications/) in premier venues across HCI, computer vision, and design research, and its alumni have gone on to leading positions in academia and industry. Interested in joining? Reach out at [cdesign-lab@purdue.edu](mailto:cdesign-lab@purdue.edu).
