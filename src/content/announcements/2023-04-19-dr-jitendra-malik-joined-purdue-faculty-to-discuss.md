<p>Dr. Jitendra Malik, a renowned computer vision and machine learning researcher, recently joined Purdue University faculty in a panel discussion moderated by Dr. Karthik Ramani, on the topic of &#8220;Is Computer Vision and Robotics amenable to ChatGPTification?&#8221; following a lecture on &#8220;Robots that can Learn and Adapt&#8221;.</p>
<p>The panel discussion explored the potential for natural language processing (NLP) to enhance computer vision and robotics. Specifically, the panel considered whether it was possible to &#8220;ChatGPTify&#8221; computer vision and robotics systems by integrating them with natural language processing capabilities.</p>
