<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/cdesign/wp/ana-villanueva/">Ana Villanueva</a> has been accepted into the Rising Stars in Mechanical Engineering Workshop at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, October 25-26, 2018.  The workshop is aimed at women graduate students and postdocs considering future careers in academia.  Ana is a D-PhD student whose research interests involve modeling human skill transfer and learning using augmented reality, data visualization, and data analysis. Other research interests include human-robot-machine interactions, machine learning, mental models for data transfer, and statistical models.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Link &#8211; Rising Starts in Mechanical Engineering: http://risingstarsme.mit.edu/</p>
