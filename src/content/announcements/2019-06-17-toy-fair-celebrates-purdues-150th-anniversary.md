<p>Officially, the ME444 class is called “Computer-Aided Design,” but everyone knows it as the <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/toydesign">Toy Design class</a>.  Students learn concept generation, CAD design, finite element analysis, and rapid prototyping, and other skills necessary to work in industry.  But all their hands-on learning takes place with toys, and their final project involves designing and building a new action toy.</p>
