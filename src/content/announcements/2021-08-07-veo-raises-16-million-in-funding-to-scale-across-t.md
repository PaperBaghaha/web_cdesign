<p><a href="https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&amp;l=en&amp;o=3225983-1&amp;h=2270501010&amp;u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.veoride.com%2F&amp;a=Veo" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Veo</a>, the first profitable micromobility company, today announced the closing of a <span class="xn-money">$16 million</span> Series A funding round led by Autotech Ventures, with participation from UP.Partners, FJ Labs, and Interplay Ventures. Veo will use the funds to expand its footprint of custom-designed, shared-use scooters and bikes to transform the way micromobility operates, while raising the standard of sustainability for the industry.</p>
<p>Headquartered in <span class="xn-location">Chicago</span> and founded in 2017, Veo is lead by two Convergence Design Lab Alumni, Candice Xie and Yanke Tan, and is one of micromobility&#8217;s fastest growing and most innovative companies.</p>
