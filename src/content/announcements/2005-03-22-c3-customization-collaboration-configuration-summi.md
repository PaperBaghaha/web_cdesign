<p><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Today research in product development, manufacturing and supply networks are converging driven by the potential use of information science and technologies. This research coupled with business insight will have a fundamental impact on your competitiveness. Our research and industry relationships are directed to enabling future paradigms by bridging the customer-design-manufacturing gaps through innovative information systems development. All companies that design and provide manufacturing services to develop their products will benefit from it. NETWORK, DISCOVER and INFLUENCE directions of the latest technologies from Purdue Research and Education Center for Information Systems in Engineering (PRECISE). PRECISE research applied to product lifecycle design and manufacturing will be used to explore how to reduce product development costs and time, create and satisfy new markets, leverage and reuse your product and manufacturing knowledge. This year our discussions on major themes will emphasize presentations and demonstrations in product lifecycle applications (PLA) followed up with thematic brainstorming. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;"><strong>This year:</strong> </span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;"> Search for parts using sketches</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Cluster parts and analyze part duplication</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Enable your customers to participate in the design process </span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Frameworks for customer-driven manufacturing networks </span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Design time supply chain design </span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Evaluate a large number of product architectural decisions at design time </span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Alter and evaluate designs across complex networks at the customers request </span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">Enable flexible and re-configurable supply-demand networks </span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: x-small;">You will be able to participate in new virtual test-bed development with partners in industry. Your enterprises will become more responsive and result in reduced logistical costs.<br />
</span></p>
