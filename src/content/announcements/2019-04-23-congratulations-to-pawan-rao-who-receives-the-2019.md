<p><b><span style="color: black;">Pawan Rao receives the 2019 Estus H. and Vashti L. Magoon Award for Excellence in Teaching for his contribution as TA for ME444 (Fall-18) and ME553 (Spring-18 &amp; 19). This award recognizes outstanding Teaching Assistants and Instructors in memory of Estus H. and Vashti L. Magoon, who have influenced the lives of many engineering educators early in their careers through this award.</span></b></p>
