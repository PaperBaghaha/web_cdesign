<p>Orlando Murcia Perdomo from the Nexo Global Program has completed his visit to the United States and left back to Colombia. Orlando was supervised by Terrell Glenn and worked in the C Design Lab, where he made many invaluable contributions to AR-IoT based projects. He presented all of his hard work to the lab and his family earlier this week, where he discussed his efforts and results.</p>
