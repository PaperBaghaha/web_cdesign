<p>The C-Design Lab has made course material from ME553 available to the world.</p>
<p>Have you ever wanted to take your ideas and communicate them visually? Do you have an idea you want to iterate on? Our <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/productdesign/wp/?page_id=330"><em>Sketch Making for Design Thinking</em> series of videos and activities</a> can help anyone learn to sketch: children, students, parents, grandparents, and more.</p>
<p>We have opened the <em>Sketching for Design Workshop</em>, from <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/productdesign/wp/">Purdue’s Product and Process Design course</a>, to help everyone learn to communicate visually.</p>
<p>All of the activities and videos are available <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/productdesign/wp/?page_id=330">here.</a></p>
