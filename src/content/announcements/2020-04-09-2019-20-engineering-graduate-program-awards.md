<p>Congratulations are in order!</p>
<p>Two of the C-Design Lab Team Members, <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/cdesign/wp/category/people/subramanian-chidambaram/">Subramanian Chidambaram</a> and <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/cdesign/wp/category/people/terrell-glenn/">Terrell Glenn</a>, were recently recognized for some of their contributions to the greater Purdue community.</p>
<p>Sub was awarded the Magoon for his contributions to <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/EPICS">EPICS</a> and Terrell the Outstanding Service Scholarship for his contributions to the School of Mechanical Engineering.</p>
<p>Congratulations again, Sub and Terrell!</p>
<hr />
<p><strong>Details about each award:</strong></p>
<p>The Purdue University College of Engineering <strong>Magoon Awards </strong>are given to outstanding teaching assistants and instructors in memory of Estus H. and Vashti L. Magoon, who have influenced the lives of many engineering educators through this award. The award is made possible through the generous Estus H. and Vashti L. Magoon endowment, and the recipients each receive $2,000 and a certificate.</p>
<p>The Purdue University College of Engineering <strong>Outstanding Service Scholarship Award</strong> recognizes the engineering graduate students who provide outstanding service to the graduate student community, the School, the College, and/or the University. Each School nominates one student, and he or she each receives $2,000 and a certificate.</p>
