<p><a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/PRECISE/people/Students/Sundar%20Murugappan">Sundar Murugappan</a> (Ph.D Student) and Professor Karthik Ramani were awarded the 2009 Prakash Krishnaswami CAPPD Best Paper Award by the American Society of Mechanical Engineering (ASME) Computers and Information in Engineering (CIE) division. Findings were detailed in a paper presented Sept. 1 during the American Society of Mechanical Engineers Design Engineering Technical Conference in San Diego. FEAsy (Finite Element Analysis Made Easy) analyzes designs from raw sketches to speed parts and product creation.
