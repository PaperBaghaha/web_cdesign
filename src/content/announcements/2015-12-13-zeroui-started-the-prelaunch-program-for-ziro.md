<p>Our lab Partner ZeroUI Inc. in Cupertino, California, (<a href="http://zeroui.com/" target="_blank">http://zeroui.com/</a>) has started the prelaunch program for Ziro! Please check <a href="http://www.ziro.io/" target="_blank">Ziro</a>Â (<a href="http://www.ziro.io/" target="_blank">http://www.ziro.io/</a>) for more details.</p>
<p>What is Ziro?</p>
<p>Ziro is a modular robotics kit made up of individual mechanized motor modules that are controlled by a wireless smart glove.</p>
<p>Design and construct your robots out of any material you have available including our pre-made starter kits. Attach the Ziro modules to animate your creations using continuous rotational or hinge movements.</p>
<p>Use the smartphone app to configure the modules using the pre-made programming templates or input your own custom gestures.</p>
<p>Control your creations remotely using the Ziro smart glove. Your hand gestures send commands to the modules so that when you move, they move.</p>
