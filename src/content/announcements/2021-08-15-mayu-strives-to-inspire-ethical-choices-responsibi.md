MAYU’s mission is to inspire ethical choices, imbibe responsibility and sustainability within luxury fashion and lifestyle without a compromise. Through their vision to build an ecosystem that inspires sustainable living, they handcraft goods for the betterment of our people and planet. With the major climate change implications of the choices we make, MAYU stands out in moving towards these sustainable choices several years ago.</p>
